Basic Level

What is the value of sin(30°)?

Show Answer

Answer: 0.5

Explanation: The sine of 30° is a standard trigonometric value that equals 1/2 or 0.5.

Intermediate Level

Find the hypotenuse if the opposite side is 5 and sin(θ) = 0.6

Show Answer

Answer: 8.33 (5/0.6)

Explanation: Since sin(θ) = opposite/hypotenuse, we can rearrange to find hypotenuse = opposite/sin(θ) = 5/0.6 ≈ 8.33

Advanced Level

Solve for x: 2sin(x) = 1 (0° < x < 180°)

Show Answer

Answer: x = 30° or 150°

Explanation: First divide both sides by 2 to get sin(x) = 0.5. The solutions in the given range are 30° and 150° (since sin(150°) also equals 0.5).
