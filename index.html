Welcome to Trigo Quest!

This game is designed to make learning trigonometry interactive and fun.

  • Engaging board game format
  • Answer questions to move forward
  • Prizes and surprises along the way

How to Play

Get ready for an exciting journey through the world of trigonometry!

  • Gather your friends and set up the game board.
  • Take turns answering questions and moving your pieces.
  • Be the first to reach the finish line and win!
