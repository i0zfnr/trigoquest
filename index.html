About Trigo Quest

Trigo Quest is an innovative board game designed to transform trigonometry learning into an exciting adventure. Developed by educators at Kota Bharu Polytechnic, it addresses common challenges students face with abstract trigonometric concepts.

The game combines gamification and active learning principles to:

  • Simplify complex topics through hands-on play
  • Encourage collaborative problem-solving
  • Provide immediate feedback through game mechanics

Game Features

Three Difficulty Levels

From basic sine/cosine to advanced identities, tailored for all learners.

Special Action Squares

BOOM! (lose turns), BONUS (extra points), and QR (video challenges).

Progress Tracking

Monitor improvement with pre- and post-game assessments.
